Here, I'll go first to break the ice:

My name is Brenton and I am the guild master for South of Heaven. I am 22 years old, male and I am Canadian. I currently reside in Waterloo, Ontario. I've been playing WoW since late Vanilla and raiding since mid BC. I have been a raider with SoH since early Wrath, the raid leader since Cata and guild master since Mists. Outside of WoW I enjoy many other games including LoL, Diablo, Hearthstone and single-player RPG games, my favourite game series being the Zelda series followed closely by Final Fantasy and Mario. Outside of gaming I am an avid reader (especially enjoy fantasy novels), watch lots TV shows and movies, am a more-than-decent skier and enjoy canoe camping.
